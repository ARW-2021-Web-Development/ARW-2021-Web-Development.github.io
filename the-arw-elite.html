The ARW Elite

Who will be the ultimate ARW Elite?

The first ever ARW Elite aims to highlight the goals, culture, and values of the 50 organizations under the Council of Student Organizations through a series of online challenges. The Ultimate ARW Elite, best embodies the character of their organization and is a great example which the Lasallian community would look up to.
