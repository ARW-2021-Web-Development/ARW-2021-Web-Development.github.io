ABOUT ARW

Annual Recruitment Week (ARW) is one of the most awaited annual activities in De La Salle University. Students are given the opportunity to choose among the 51 organizations under the Council of Student Organizations in pursuit of being able to meet people of similar interests and hone their skills outside of academic responsibilities.

For this year’s highlight, a university-wide pageant entitled “Mr. and Mr. ARW 2021” wherein there will be representatives whose goal is not only to showcase their wits and elegance, but most especially the beauty of their respective organizations in pursuit of enticing more applicants.
